DO NOT ADJUST THE CURRENT CONTROL KNOB ON THE POWER SUPPLY.

Disconnect power from the breadboard when connecting/disconnecting elements on the breadboard. You can turn off the power supply or disconnect one of the wires from the board.

FOR LAB ACTIVITY 2: Do not exceed 15V on the power supply.

CAUTION: RESISTORS AND LIGHT BULB WILL GET HOT when power is ON for a long time.
